We Are Digging Into The Future

Be part of our Washington Tunneling Club that revolutionizes the future of the transportation system.

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness brilliant minds of University of Washington students to engineer innovative transit solutions.

We are on the journey of building a Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) that robustly excavates tunnels, the foundation of Hyperloop.

Our Vision

Our vision is to strive for a sustainable society by designing rapid transit system through constructing a Tunnel Boring Machine.

Like the University of Washington's motto: "LUX SIT" or "Let there be light", we pursue innovative solutions that creates positive impact in our world,

We train and enhance our future engineers with various backgrounds to solve solutions and help them gain hands-on experiences.
